Frequently asked questions

Find answers to common questions about our astrology services.

1. How accurate are your astrology readings?

+
Our astrology readings are based on time-tested calculations using your birth details. While we strive for accuracy, predictions are interpretive and may vary based on external factors..

2. What information do I need to provide for a reading?

+
You’ll need to provide your full birth date, time, and location to generate an accurate natal chart and personalized prediction.

3. Can I speak to a live astrologer?

+
Yes! We offer one-on-one consultations with certified astrologers. You can schedule a session directly through our website.

4. Is my data secure?

+
Yes, all your personal details are encrypted and handled securely. We do not share or sell your information to third parties.

5. Do you offer refunds?

+
Due to the personalized nature of our services, refunds are generally not provided unless there's a technical issue. Please contact support for help.

6. What languages are available?

+
Currently, our services are available in English. We're working on adding support for regional languages soon.
