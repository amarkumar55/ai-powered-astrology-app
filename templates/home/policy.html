🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AstroLive collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

We may collect the following types of personal information:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. While no system is 100% secure, we strive to keep your personal information safe from unauthorized access.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share it with trusted service providers (e.g., payment gateways, email platforms) solely for the purpose of operating our services.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. We may also use analytics tools like Google Analytics to track usage patterns. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for processing your data
  • Request a copy of your data

Please contact us at support@astrolive.com for any such requests.

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.
