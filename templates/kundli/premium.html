🔮 Kundli Yoga Overview

🔥 Maha Purush Yogas

🌕 What is Maha Purush Yogas?
These powerful yogas are formed when a planet is in its own or exalted sign and placed in a Kendra (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th house). They bring strength, talent, and success. Here's a quick breakdown:

  • Ruchaka Yoga – Mars in own/exalted sign in Kendra: Courage, leadership, strength.
  • Bhadra Yoga – Mercury in own/exalted sign in Kendra: Intelligence, charm, communication.
  • Hamsa Yoga – Jupiter in own/exalted sign in Kendra: Wisdom, spirituality, wealth.
  • Malavya Yoga – Venus in own/exalted sign in Kendra: Beauty, luxury, relationships.
  • Shasha Yoga – Saturn in own/exalted sign in Kendra: Authority, discipline, success.
